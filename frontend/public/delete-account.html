You can request deletion of your Vivrecon account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion
Email nataljapolozun@gmail.com from the email address on your account, with the subject “Delete my account”. We will verify your request and delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Your account and all data you entered — profile, budgets, income, expenses, accounts, debts, savings goals, subscriptions, and all other records. This deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What we may keep

We may retain limited records where required by law (for example, payment/transaction records related to a Premium subscription), for the minimum period the law requires. Receipt photos are processed on your device and are never stored on our servers.
