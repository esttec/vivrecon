Study & educational notice. Vivrecon is provided for educational and study purposes. It is a personal budgeting tool and does not provide financial, investment, legal, or tax advice. You are responsible for the data you enter and the decisions you make.

This Privacy Policy explains how Vivrecon (“Vivrecon”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Vivrecon website and mobile application (together, the “Service”). By using the Service you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Vivrecon is operated by the Vivrecon team. For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at nataljapolozun@gmail.com. We act as the data controller for the personal data described below.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information you provide: email address, display name, chosen currency, and password (stored only in encrypted/hashed form).
  • Approximate location: your country and IP address at the time you create your account, used to set your default language and for security.
  • Financial data you enter: budgets, income, expenses, accounts and balances, debts, savings goals, subscriptions, household, clothing, travel and children-related expenses, and similar entries you choose to record.
  • Imported statements: if you use the import feature, the bank statement file you upload is processed to create budget entries.
  • Receipt scanning: receipt photos are processed on your device to read the total; the images are not uploaded to or stored on our servers.
  • Usage & device data: basic technical information (such as browser or app version) needed to operate and secure the Service.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the budgeting features and store the data you enter.
  • To create and secure your account and keep you signed in.
  • To process Premium subscription payments.
  • To set your language and improve the Service.
  • To show advertising to users on the free plan (see section 5).

Our legal bases (where GDPR applies) are performance of our agreement with you, your consent (for advertising cookies), and our legitimate interest in operating and securing the Service.

4. Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed by Stripe. When you subscribe, your payment details are entered on Stripe’s secure checkout and handled by Stripe — we do not receive or store your full card number. Stripe processes this data as an independent controller/processor under its own privacy policy (stripe.com/privacy). We receive limited information such as your subscription status and renewal date.

5. Advertising (free plan)

If you use the free plan, we display ads through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve and measure ads. In the European Economic Area, the UK, and Switzerland, we ask for your consent through a Google-certified consent message before non-essential advertising cookies are used, in line with Google’s requirements. Paid (Premium) subscribers are not shown ads. You can learn more at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

6. Cookies & local storage

We use local storage to keep you signed in and remember preferences such as your language. Advertising partners may set cookies as described above, subject to your consent where required.

7. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers that help us run the Service, such as our hosting provider, Stripe (payments), and Google (advertising). These providers process data on our behalf or as independent controllers under their own policies. We may also disclose data if required by law.

8. Data retention

We keep your account and the data you enter for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us; we will delete it unless we are legally required to keep certain records.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, to withdraw consent, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email nataljapolozun@gmail.com. If you are in the EEA/UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including HTTPS encryption in transit and hashed passwords, to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. The “Children” feature is a tool for parents to track their own household expenses and does not create accounts for children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

12. International transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located in the European Union and by service providers in other countries. Where data is transferred internationally, appropriate safeguards are applied.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will change the “Last updated” date above and, for significant changes, provide additional notice where appropriate.
